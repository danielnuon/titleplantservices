WHAT WE DO

TPS owns title plants across the Northwest and Hawaii and has access to title data nationwide. We make these plants available for purchase, lease, and subscription.

INDEXING

We index both our own title plants and those for others to streamline searches and improve accessibility.

PURCHASING TITLE PLANTS

We purchase title plants from across the United States, expanding our network and resources.

VALUATIONS AND APPRAISALS

We assist buyers and sellers of title plants in establishing their value, offering professional appraisals.

EVALUATION AND AUDITING

We help evaluate title plants, supporting underwriters and state audits to ensure compliance and accuracy.

MARKETPLACE ACCESS

We provide a marketplace where title plant buyers and sellers can connect, browse, and shop for plants.
