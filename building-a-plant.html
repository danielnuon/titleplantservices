HOW WE BUILD A PLANT

RESEARCH AND PLANNING

We evaluate the number of recorded documents, and how they are stored by the repository (typically the county). We determine how best to obtain images of the documents.

DATA COLLECTION

We collect the images and place them on our servers for easy access and long-term storage.

ORGANIZING AND INDEXING

We manually review and index each document, extracting key information to streamline future searches.

TITLE PLANT SOFTWARE

We recommend the best title plant software to expedite searches and optimize your plant's functionality.

MAINTENANCE AND UPDATES

We keep your title plant up-to-date by adding newly recorded documents, ensuring the plant remains accurate and efficient.

QUALITY CONTROL

We perform audits and quality control to ensure the accuracy of your title plant and meet underwriter requirements.
