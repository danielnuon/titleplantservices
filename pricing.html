PRICING

What does a Title Plant cost?

Cost is directly related to the number of documents in the plant. A good rule of thumb is to budget from $0.35 to $0.60 per document to purchase a plant.

Is purchasing a Title Plant worth the investment?

Writing title insurance can be a very lucrative business. If you are a title agency in an adjoining county, we can quickly get you licensed and legal in neighboring counties. If you are a real estate broker, you can direct business to your own agency. We can show you how to do this and be fully RESPA compliant.

Can you lease a Title Plant, or purchase on terms?

Yes. We are happy to get you quickly up and running in business. Some ways to do that:

  • Purchase, and spread the payments over time: You own the plant.
  • Lease for a monthly fee: We own the plant, you get unlimited use of the plant during the lease term.
  • Subscribe to the plant: We do this in some counties. We own the plant, and you pay just for your use. This option might make sense if you're starting with limited title orders.
